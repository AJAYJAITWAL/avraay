About Us

Pioneering Air Filtration Solutions

FiltraLux is a globally recognized manufacturer of premium fiberglass filter fabrics, serving the industrial air filtration sector since 2005. Our state-of-the-art production facilities span over 50,000 square meters.

We specialize in high-temperature resistant filter media for pulse-jet baghouses, flue gas desulfurization, and cement kiln dust collection. Every meter of fabric undergoes rigorous quality testing.
